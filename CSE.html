    • College of Science and Engineering

    The College of Science and Engineering was established in 1995 to provide the foundation for all fields of knowledge, and was one of the founding colleges at National Dong Hwa University. With eight departments, the College offers exceptional degree programs for undergraduate and graduate studies. In addition, there are five centers to support the teaching and research functions. The college seeks to make itself the school of choice for higher education and advanced research by promoting cross-disciplinary activities. We currently have about 1,900 undergraduate and 400 graduate students and 110 faculties
